## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-09-02-T1 | Tampering | scraped body_html (stored XSS vector) | mitigate | `_clean_wikipedia_html` strips `<script>`, `style`, and `on*` event-handler attributes, and removes `data:` src/href values before storage. Blog HTML removes script/style via `_BLOG_NOISE_SELECTORS`. Render-side mitigation in 09-03. |
